Higher National Diploma in Software Engineering

Higher National Diploma in Software Engineering

Our university's HND program offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to equip students with the skills needed in today's ever-evolving IT landscape. From Robotics Application Developments and Programming to Advanced Database Management Systems and Software Security, students gain practical experience in developing applications for robotic systems, mastering programming languages, and addressing complex database challenges.

The program also explores cutting-edge technologies such as the Internet of Things and Machine Learning for Artificial Intelligence. Practical components like Mobile Application Development, Data Warehousing and Data Mining, and an Innovations and Entrepreneurship Project provide hands-on experience. The program culminates in an industry placement, ensuring students are well-prepared for successful careers in IT.
