Diploma in Management Information Systems

Diploma in Management Information Systems

The Diploma in Management Information Systems equips students for IT careers through a comprehensive curriculum. Core courses like Introduction to Information Systems and Programming (.NET & Java) build a foundation. Business Data Analytics (Power BI), E-Commerce & Digital Marketing, ERP & Web Development focus on practical business applications. Project Management, Cyber Security & Cloud Computing, and Quality Assurance cover essential management and control aspects. A System Design project caps the program, preparing graduates for diverse information systems roles.

Entry Requirements

  • A/L (Sri Lanka / London) – Two passes in any stream
  • O/L (Sri Lanka / London) – Five credits including Maths and English / ICT
