BA (Hons) Management and Leadership

BA (Hons) Management and Leadership

Whether you want to run your own business or aspire to become the manager of a large company, management roles are both challenging and rewarding. The BA (Hons) Management & Leadership degree, awarded by Coventry University is designed to help you develop key leadership skills, from managing people and resources to strategic planning.

The degree focuses on contemporary management issues, giving you the opportunity to develop a wealth of general skills needed to aid progression from an operational level through to leadership.

Success in this field requires confidence, versatility and strategic thinking, coupled with elevated communication skills and a desire to succeed.

Upon completion of this degree, you will receive a qualification awarded by Coventry University.

Entry Requirements

For entry into Year 1: Diploma in Business Management

  • Those who have sat for GCE Advanced Level and qualified with minimum Three (03) simple passes are eligible for the first year of the degree program
  • AND
  • Those who have sat for GCE Advanced Level and absence of Three (03) simple passes should need to follow Certification in Business Management (CBM) during the First year of the degree program
  • OR
  • Those who have completed their GCE Ordinary level will have to follow Certificate Course in Business Management and after successful completion are eligible for the first year of the degree program

For entry into Year 2: Higher National Diploma in Business Management

  • Those who have successfully completed the course work of the Diploma in Business Management program are eligible to follow Higher National Diploma in Business Management
  • AND
  • Students should be qualified in all the modules of Diploma in Business Management during the Second Year to be qualified for Higher National Diploma in Business Management

For entry into Year 3: BA (Hons) Management and Leadership

  • Those who have successfully qualified in Diploma in Business Management and Higher National Diploma in Business Management are eligible for the Final Year Top up degree in BA (Hons) Management and Leadership
